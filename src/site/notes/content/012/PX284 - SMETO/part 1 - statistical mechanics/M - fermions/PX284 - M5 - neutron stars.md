- considering a neutron star: $m = m_n$, $n \sim 10^{44}$ m$^{-3}$, and $s = 1/2$
$$\begin{gather}
E_{F} \sim 40\,\text{MeV}\\
T_{F} \sim 5\times10^{11}\,\text{K}\\
p_{n} \sim 3\times10^{32}\,\text{Pa}
\end{gather}$$
- this degeneracy pressure keeps the star from collapsing further
