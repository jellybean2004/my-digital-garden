## density of states
$$g(k) \, dk = \frac{Vk^{2}}{2\pi^{2}} dk \times 2$$
	where, the factor of 2 arises from the spin degeneracy ($2s+1 =2$ for $s =1/2$)
$$E = \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{2}}{2m}$$
$$\begin{gather}
k = \sqrt{\frac{2mE}{\hbar^{2}}} \\
dk = \sqrt{\frac{2m}{\hbar^{2}}} \frac{1}{2\sqrt{E}} \,dE
\end{gather}$$
