- similarly, expressions for other thermodynamic potentials
-  pressure:
$$p = - \left(\frac{\partial F}{\partial V} \right) _{T}$$
- enthalpy: 
$$H = U + pV$$
- gibb's free energy: 
$$G = H - TS$$
- $Z$ can be used to find $U$ and $F$, and hence find ${} S:$ 
$$ S= \frac{U-F}{T}$$
- also, heat capacity: 
$$C_{V} = \left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial T}\right)_{V}$$
