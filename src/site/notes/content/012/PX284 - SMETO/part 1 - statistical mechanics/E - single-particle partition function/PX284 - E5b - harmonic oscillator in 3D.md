- the energy is given by:
$$E = \left(n_{x} + \frac{1}{2}\right) \hbar \omega +  \left(n_{y} + \frac{1}{2}\right) \hbar \omega +  \left(n_{z} + \frac{1}{2}\right) \hbar \omega$$
- the partition function:
$$Z_{3D} = Z_{x} Z_{y} Z_{z} = (Z_{1D})^{3}$$
- the internal energy:
$$U_{3D} = 3\,U_{1D} $$
