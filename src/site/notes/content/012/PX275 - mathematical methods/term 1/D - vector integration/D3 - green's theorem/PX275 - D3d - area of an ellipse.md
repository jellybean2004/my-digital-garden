$$\begin{align*}
	x &= a\cos\phi \\
	y &= b\sin\phi \\
	&0 \leq \phi\leq 2\pi
\end{align*}$$
taking the differentials of $x$ and $y:$ 
$$\begin{align*}
	dx &= - a\sin\phi\,d\phi \\
	dy &= b\cos\phi\,d\phi
\end{align*}$$
