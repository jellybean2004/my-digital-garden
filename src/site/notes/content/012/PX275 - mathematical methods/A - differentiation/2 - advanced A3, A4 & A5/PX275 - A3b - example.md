- consider a function: 
$$f = (x+y)^{2}$$
- it follows a path that varies in time such that: $x=t;\; y=\sin t$
- using the chain rule: 
$$\frac{df}{dt} = 2(x+y) + 2(x+y)\cos t = 2(t+\sin t) (1 + \cos t)$$
