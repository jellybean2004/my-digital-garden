\end{gather}$$
- the inertia matrix:
$$M = \begin{pmatrix}m_{O} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & m_{C}& 0 \\ 0 & 0 & m_{O}\end{pmatrix}$$
- the stiffness matrix:
$$K = \begin{pmatrix}k & -k & 0 \\ -k & 2k & -k \\ 0 & -k & k\end{pmatrix}$$
