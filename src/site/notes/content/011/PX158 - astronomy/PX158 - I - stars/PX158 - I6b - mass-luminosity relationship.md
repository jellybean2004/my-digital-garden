$$\begin{align*}
	t &= \frac{E}{L} \\
	E &\propto M \\
	L &\propto M^{4} \\
	\therefore t &\propto M^{-3}
\end{align*}$$

- for a star of mass, $10\,M_{\odot}: t\approx 10\,Myr$ 
- for a star of mass, $1\,M_{\odot}: t\approx 10-12\,Gyr$ 
- for a star of mass, $0.1\,M_{\odot}: t\approx 6000\,Gyr$
