- energy is quantized: 
$$E_{n}= n\,h\,f$$
	where, $n\in\,\mathbb{Z}$, $h=$ planck's constant
- average energy: 
$$\langle E\rangle = \frac{hf}{\exp({\frac{hf}{k_{B}T}})-1}$$
