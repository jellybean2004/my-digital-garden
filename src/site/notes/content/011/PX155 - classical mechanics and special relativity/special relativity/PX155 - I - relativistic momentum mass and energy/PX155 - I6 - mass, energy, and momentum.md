- key relations: 
$$\vec p = \gamma m_{0} \vec v$$
$$E = \gamma m_{0} c^{2}$$
- consider: $$E^{2}-p^{2}c^{2} = \gamma ^{2}m_{0}^{2}c^{4}\left(1- \frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}\right)=  \frac{\gamma^{2}m_{0}^{2}c^{4}}{\gamma^{2}}$$
$$\therefore E^{2}- p^{2}c^{2} = m_{0}^{2}c^{4}$$
	- $RHS$ is frame invariant - *lorentz invariant*
